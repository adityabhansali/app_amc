About us

Protecting Gujarat's buildings — one maintained system at a time.

Northern Star Engineering is a Surat-based fire & safety engineering company delivering 360° fire protection — from system design and installation to ongoing maintenance, emergency response, and Fire NOC clearance.

Founded in Surat, Gujarat · 7+ years of experience · NBC · BIS · IS · NFPA compliant
Our mission

Fire safety should never fail because maintenance was skipped.

Most fire systems do not fail because they were never installed — they fail because they were never maintained. Empty extinguishers. Pressure lost. Powder turned solid. Alarms disconnected.

Northern Star was built to solve that. We are the maintenance-first company — on-call, on-schedule, and on-record for every system we look after. Our goal is a Gujarat where no building catches fire because its safety systems were not working.

24×7 availability

Fire emergencies do not follow business hours. Neither do we.

Full compliance

All work engineered to NBC, BIS, IS and NFPA codes and local fire department regulations.

Every visit documented

Photos, work-done notes, and a signed service report for every single visit — visible in your portal.
